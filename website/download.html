Download

B4wbem is available via cvs and as tarballs. For the packages to work you need a couple of non standard perl modules. Right now you should figure out yourself, which modules are needed, but I will come up with a bundle of the modules sometime.
Furthermore, you need a properly installed nsgmls. It's included in either of the RPMs "jade" (RedHat) or "sp" (Debian and SuSE).
